Hi I Am BayMax

Baymax is wearable heart rate sensor has a number of potential applications in healthcare, particularly in the area of remote patient monitoring. By tracking a person's heart rate over time, doctors and other healthcare providers can identify patterns and trends that may indicate potential health problems, allowing for early intervention and treatment. in a potential health issue Baymax sends the location of the patient to the nearest hospital so as fast as possible the hospital sends an ambulance to the patient location and make the hospital provides a Reserved bed for that patient
